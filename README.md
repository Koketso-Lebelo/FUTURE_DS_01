# Business Objective

## Business Problem
The company wants to understand its sales performance using transaction data in order to identify revenue trends, top-performing products, customer behavior, and geographic sales opportunities.

## Project Objective
To analyze online retail sales transactions and build a business dashboard that helps decision-makers understand:
revenue performance over time
top-selling products
best-performing countries
customer purchasing behavior
return/cancellation patterns
